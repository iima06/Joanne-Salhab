About

Graduated from the Atman School of Osteopathy in 2015, I practice holistic osteopathy focused on listening, respecting the body, and considering the patient as a whole. Each consultation begins with a real conversation: understanding your story, medical background, and your sensations.

How a session works

Each session includes a detailed history, osteopathic testing, and a manual treatment adapted to your needs. Techniques used include structural, functional, visceral, cranial, fascial, and tendinous. For example, osteopathy can help relieve back pain, sprains, stress, menstrual pain, or migraines. Osteopathy is both preventive and curative.
