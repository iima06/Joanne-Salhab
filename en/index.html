FAQ

How long does a session last?

A consultation lasts approximately 45 minutes to 1 hour, depending on your reason for consultation and your needs.

How many sessions are needed?

The number of sessions depends on your condition, your age, and how your symptoms evolve. Sometimes one session is enough, sometimes several spaced sessions are necessary for lasting results. We will evaluate this together step by step.

Can my baby benefit from a session?

Yes, I treat newborns from their first days of life, gently and with adapted techniques. Examples: plagiocephaly, reflux, colic, torticollis, sucking or sleeping difficultiesâ€¦

Does osteopathy hurt?

No. The osteopathy I practice is gentle and respectful of the body. If any technique could cause discomfort, I always inform you beforehand.

Do I need a prescription to consult?

No, osteopathy is a direct-access healthcare: you can consult without a medical prescription.

Is osteopathy reimbursed?

It is not reimbursed by the Social Security, but most health insurances partially or fully cover sessions. I provide an invoice at the end of the consultation.

What is the difference between an osteopath and a physiotherapist?

An osteopath works independently, without prescription, and with a global vision of the body. They use gentle manual techniques to restore balance and relieve tensions. A physiotherapist usually works under medical prescription, after an injury or surgery, with a rehabilitation program and exercises. Both approaches are complementary.
